Food, Fun, Family A Little More About Me

I have lived in Southern California for most of my life, but am now studying marketing at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. Looking back, long before I stepped foot on BYU's campus or even ventured into the videography/photography world, my first sentence (ever!) was “Did you see that?” This may seem so insignificant, but I like to think that this was the first evidence of my desire to capture cool moments. :) I distinctly remember becoming interested in videography when I was 12 years old as I made a quick video for my youth group at church. No surprise, but I loved the project. Since that moment, photography and videography have been large parts of my life. I am always learning, but I love the process! Everyone has a story and I love that I get to capture others' adventures, experiences, and their own “did you see that?” moments.

Beyond work, I am an avid cookie dough and Reese's Peanut Butter Cup connoisseur, a big fan of the Netflix series "Stranger Things", and a lover of the sun and soccer. I love spending time with my loved ones and having a good time. I am so grateful for all the wonderful people in my life that have led me here today.
